# Privacy Policy — EmailDigest

## What we collect
- Your Gmail access token (stored only on your device)
- Your email subjects, senders and content (processed temporarily for AI summarization)

## What we DO NOT do
- We never store your emails on our servers
- We never send emails on your behalf
- We never share your data with third parties
- We never sell your data

## How to verify
- Our full source code is public at github.com/mnooman2018/emaildigest
- We use Gmail's readonly scope — we physically cannot send or delete emails
- You can logout anytime to revoke all access

## Data deletion
Clicking logout immediately removes all tokens from your device.
